UniMind Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how UniMind (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application, website, and related services. By using UniMind, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: email, name, and credentials you provide when you sign up or sign in.
  • Profile and preferences: goals, domains (e.g. health, productivity, finance, lifestyle), and settings you choose in the app.
  • Usage and content: tasks, steps, budgets, notes, chat messages, and other inputs you voluntarily add to use UniMind’s features.
  • Device & technical data: basic device or browser information needed for the service to work securely (e.g. session tokens, app version).

2. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account and workspace.
  • To provide core features: planning, tracking, insights, notifications, and AI-assisted tips where enabled.
  • To improve reliability, security, and user experience.
  • To communicate with you about the service when necessary (e.g. password recovery, important notices).

3. AI features

Some UniMind features use artificial intelligence (e.g. suggestions or chat). Prompts you send may be processed by our backend and, where applicable, by third-party AI providers under their terms. We do not use your data to train public models unless we clearly tell you otherwise and you agree.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only:

  • With service providers who help us host, analyze, or secure the service (under strict confidentiality).
  • When required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • With your explicit consent.

5. Data retention

We keep your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account or certain data where applicable law allows.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we encourage you to use a strong password and keep your credentials private.

7. Your choices

  • Access or update profile information in the app or on the website where supported.
  • Adjust permissions (e.g. notifications, health-related features) in your device settings.
  • Contact us to ask about access, correction, or deletion, subject to applicable law.

8. Children’s privacy

UniMind is not directed at children under the age where parental consent is required in your region. We do not knowingly collect personal information from those children.

9. International users

Your information may be processed in countries where we or our providers operate. By using UniMind, you understand that transfers may occur as permitted by law.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the new version with an updated date. Continued use after changes means you accept the revised policy where allowed by law.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us using the support channel shown in the UniMind app under Help or Settings, or at the official contact email provided there.
